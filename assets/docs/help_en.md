# Player Guide - Galad Islands Rail Shooter

## Game Controls

### Player Movement

- **W** or **Up Arrow** : Move Up
- **S** or **Down Arrow** : Move Down
- **A** or **Left Arrow** : Move Left  
- **D** or **Right Arrow** : Move Right

### Combat

- **Space** : Fire
- **Mouse Click** : Fire (alternative)

### Menu

- **ESC** : Exit game
- **Enter/Space** : Confirm menu choices

## Objective

Survive as long as possible by destroying waves of enemies that appear!

### Enemy Types

#### **Scout**
Fast enemy that zigzags across the screen. Easy to hit but unpredictable.
- **Points** : 10
- **Speed** : Fast
- **Behavior** : Zigzags while shooting

#### **Marauder** 
Enemy that fires in bursts and follows the player. More dangerous but slower.
- **Points** : 10
- **Speed** : Medium  
- **Behavior** : Fires bursts of 3 projectiles

#### **Kamikaze**
Enemy that charges directly at you! Eliminate quickly.
- **Points** : 10
- **Speed** : Variable (accelerates near player)
- **Behavior** : Charges directly toward player

## Gameplay

### Scoring System
- Each destroyed enemy awards **10 points**
- Survive as long as possible for a high score
- Enter your name in the leaderboard after the game

### Name Entry (Arcade Cabinet)
After a game, use these controls to enter your name:
- **↑** : Change letter up (A→B→C...)
- **↓** : Change letter down (Z→Y→X...)
- **→** : Confirm letter and move to next
- **←** : Go back to previous letter
- **Enter/Space** : Confirm complete name
- **Backspace** : Delete current letter

### Enemy Waves
- Enemies appear in **continuous waves**
- Maximum **8 enemies** on screen simultaneously
- Difficulty increases progressively  
- Different enemy types with unique behaviors

### Survival Tips
1. **Stay moving** - Enemies aim at your position
2. **Manage space** - Don't get cornered
3. **Priorities** - Eliminate Kamikazes first
4. **Fire rate** - Shoot continuously, no ammo limit
5. **Anticipate** - Watch enemy movement patterns

## Keyboard Navigation

### Main Menu
- **↑/↓** or **W/S** : Navigate between options
- **Enter/Space** : Select option
- **ESC** : Exit game
